# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Potential employers, high-end clients, technical collaborators, and the creative tech community. They are looking for a partner who can bridge the gap between deep engineering and exceptional aesthetic execution.

## Product Purpose
To transition Federica Mariani's professional presence from a static CV to a high-performance, immersive "Proof of Concept." It serves as a living demonstration of technical mastery (React 19, Next.js), innovation leadership (AI-native products), and a sophisticated design sensibility.

## Brand Personality
Dark, minimalist, and brutalist. The tone is sophisticated, technical, and opinionated. It values "kinetic precision"—motion that feels weighted and intentional, rather than decorative or playful.

## Anti-references
- **Generic SaaS Templates**: Avoid the "safe" cream/white backgrounds and rounded icons.
- **Standard Resume Sites**: No linear, text-heavy layouts that feel like a digital document.
- **Playful/Bouncy Animations**: No elastic easing or "fun" bounces. Motion must feel mechanical and physical.

## Design Principles
- **Design IS the Product**: Every interaction is a testimony to engineering quality.
- **Kinetic Precision**: Motion must have weight, velocity, and purpose (driven by GSAP).
- **Ink over Void**: Use deep, sophisticated neutrals (#0E0E0E) instead of pure #000 for a premium feel.
- **Brutalist Scale**: Use high-contrast typography (Clash Display) to create impact without clutter.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **Reduced Motion**: Provide a graceful, high-quality fallback or simplified transitions for users with `prefers-reduced-motion` enabled.
- **Readability**: Ensure high-contrast ratios for off-white typography on dark backgrounds.
